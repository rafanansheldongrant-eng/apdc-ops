Daily Operations Guide — What to Check & When

Morning (7:00 – 7:30 AM, Manila time / 1:00 – 1:30 PM, HST)
Check form submissions, mark attendance (Present/Absent/Late/Half Day), review new work orders from all sources (client website, email, Messenger).

Midday (12:00 – 12:30 PM, Manila / 6:00 – 6:30 PM, HST)
Check work reports, reply to urgent messages, update work order statuses, review PMS schedule for upcoming tasks.

Evening (5:30 – 6:00 PM, Manila / 11:30 PM – 12:00 AM, HST)
Review end-of-day forms and GPS check-ins, enter building scores, send worker feedback and reminders.

Every Monday
Submit weekly operations report + proposal. Submit additional costs for billing. Deadline: every Monday.

15th & Last Day of Month
Submit bi-monthly operations report to client.

SOP Reminders: Do NOT use Zonrox bleach in chapel areas. CR deep clean weekly. Full deep clean twice yearly. Inspection visits twice monthly.
